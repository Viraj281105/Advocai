# INDIAN LEGAL KNOWLEDGE BASE (IRDAI, CPA & OMBUDSMAN)

## 1. CLAIM SETTLEMENT & INTEREST (The "30 Day" Rule)
IRDAI Regulations (2017) Reg 15(10):
- Insurer must settle or reject a claim within 30 days.
- Investigation max timeline = 45 days.
- Penalty interest = 2% above Bank Rate.

## 2. MORATORIUM PERIOD (5 Year Clause)
IRDAI Master Circular 2024 (Clause 4.2):
- After 60 months of coverage, claim cannot be rejected for non-disclosure unless fraud is proven.

## 3. CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT 2019
Wrongful claim denial = Deficiency in Service.

## 5. STANDARDIZATION OF EXCLUSIONS & FAIR PRACTICE
IRDAI Circular on Plain Language (2018):
- Ambiguities in policy terms (like 'experimental' or 'unproven') must be interpreted in favor of the policyholder (Contra Proferentem Rule). If clinical evidence suggests utility, the denial based on policy ambiguity is voidable.
- Modern treatments like Robotic Surgery must be covered if medically necessary.

## 6. POLICYWORDING: EXCLUSIONS
- Policy exclusions (like 'experimental') must be clearly defined and should not override general medical necessity unless explicitly required by statute.
